Presentation of Data-Set

Observations at the datasets are the 81 cities in Turkey.There are 42 variable to measure of life satisfaction.There are total of 3402 observations at the dataset.There are no missing values. variables at the dataset are continuous numerical variables.

  • X2: Toilet availability rate in the residence

  • X12: life expectancy at birth
